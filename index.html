FEE REFUNDS: A FIX FOR PROFITABILITY & TRUST

1. The Problem

Manual, inconsistent fee refunds fuel customer frustration, damage trust, and create significant regulatory risk. The current process is slow, inefficient, and lacks the data needed for root cause analysis.

2. Urgency: Why Act Now?

Unfair fees are a top driver of customer churn and a primary target for regulators. Proactive reform strengthens our market position and mitigates costly compliance actions.
