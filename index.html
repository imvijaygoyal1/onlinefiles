A Strategic Imperative: Transforming the Fee Refund Experience

An investment to enhance customer trust, drive operational efficiency, and protect our bottom line.

The Problem: A Broken Process Eroding Trust

Our current non-standard fee refund process is a critical pain point. It requires customers to visit a branch, where bankers face long hold times of **10-20 minutes** on support lines. This inefficient, inconsistent experience frustrates both customers and staff, damaging brand loyalty.

The Recommended Solution: Digital First, Data Driven

Our strategy is a two-pronged approach that combines a modern, self-service digital solution with clear policy improvements. This addresses both the immediate customer experience and the long-term root causes of bank errors.

How It Works: The New Customer Journey

1. Request

Customer initiates refund via app/website using a simple form or AI chatbot.

→
2. Instant Resolve

AI validates request against new, clear policies. Eligible refunds are processed instantly.

→
3. Escalate

Complex cases are automatically routed to a specialized team for quick review.

→
4. Analyze

All data is logged, enabling root cause analysis to prevent future errors systemically.

Why Now? The Cost of Inaction

Market pressures and regulatory scrutiny on "junk fees" are intensifying. Fees are a primary driver of customer attrition. Failing to act decisively risks significant market share loss and reputational damage as competitors streamline their own customer service experiences.

We must lead the market in fairness and transparency to retain our customer base and strengthen our competitive position.
